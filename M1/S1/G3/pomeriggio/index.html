Focaccia with caramelized onions,cranberrier, and brie

Wow your raste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onionsm tart cranberries and creamy brie. It wull become new your favorite holiday starter!

Featured in:

Frash Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onioins, thinly slice
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cube
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In medium-sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingrediesnts untill the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
